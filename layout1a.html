The Flex Layout module is new layout module in CSS3 made to improve the items align,
directions and order in the container even when they are with dynamic or even unknown size.
The prime characteristic of the flex container is the ability to modify the width or height of its children
to fill the available space in the best possible way on different screen sizes.
